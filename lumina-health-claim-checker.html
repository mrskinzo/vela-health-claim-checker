How we rate health claims

This tool evaluates health claims against peer-reviewed medical literature and clinical guidelines. Here's what each rating means:

Strong Evidence Multiple peer-reviewed RCTs or Cochrane meta-analyses. Clinically validated.
Limited Evidence Some clinical support but inconsistent, small-scale, or indirect studies.
No Evidence No peer-reviewed studies support this claim.
Potentially Harmful Clinical guidelines advise against this. Seek medical care immediately.
